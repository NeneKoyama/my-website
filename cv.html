CV

nene koyama / 古山寧々

　

Profile

I was born in 1999 in Gifu Prefecture and graduated from Tama Art University's Media Arts course, continuing to its graduate school.
My work, exploring "human existence from non-human perspectives," earned me the 29th Student CG Awards and the 2023 Outstanding Graduation Project Award.
Recently, I've exhibited at the ICC Kids Program 2023, TUB showing 2023, and the Space Design Conference.
I love love univerese.
